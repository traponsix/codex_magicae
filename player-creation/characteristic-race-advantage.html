Characteristics race options


You can choose one between the following options and the number of characteristics you select to be added as bonus/malus to every character of your race, you need to specify the characteristics to which that should apply (For, Des...)



Example:


n of characteristics to change changing value
2 1
1 -1
Cost: 2

for example in this case you need to add 1 (second column) to 2 different characteristics (first column), and add -1 to 1 other (different) characteristics, with this specific build you're gonna modify a total of 3 (2+1) characteristics for a cost of 2 RCP (last row).



PLEASE NOTE:
You cannot buy a second Characteristic race advantage, only one per race
