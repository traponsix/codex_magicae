Ability bonus

1 RCP



This race posses a natural skill in a certain ability,
Give you a +2 bonus to specifics abilities of your choosing, this bonus is cumulative.



Insensible

4 RCP



This race does not posses a nervous system, therefore it does not notice when it get damaged; This trait does not change how LP works or how this character is afflicted by damage.



Absorbtion

6 RCP



This race have a very thick layer of muscle or fat, so much so that you get a damage reduction of +1 for physical damage



Blood magic

8 RCP



This race posses a predetermined magic in their blood, giving them the possibility to cast that magic when they want


I still need to work out how magic works, for now just invent a spell if you want to use it

Camouflage

12 RCP



This race posses the ability to change their skin color at will, giving them a +10 on stealth, +5 on deception to fake being someone else and +2 on intimidation



Claws

4 RCP



This race posses claws, they can be used as natural weapon and deal 1d4 slashing damage



Cold blood

-6 RCP



This race cannot warm himself up, they will have a better resistance in warm ambience, but they will exponentially slow down their movement until they cannot move at all when the temperature get below 0 Celsius degrees.
They will also have to spend time under the sun for at least 3 hours per day, or they will be slowed down



Digger

10 RCP



This race posses the ability to travel underground by digging tunnels, their underground speed will be:

[(character movement speed * Strenth modifier) / 9]

PLEASE NOTE: Strength modifier will always be considered at least 1, therefore it count only if it's of 2 or more



Elemental resistance

3 RCP



This race posses a natural resistance to a certain type of element, you can choose one element when creating the race and all damage from that tpe will be halved.
You can get this traits multiple times, either to get resistance to multiple elements or if you take the same elements twice, the race will be completely invulnerable to that element.



Exceptional sense

1 RCP



This race has a +5 on perception



Exceptional vision

1 RCP

Requires: exceptional sense

In normal condition, this race can see + 5km more then the normal vision (5km) effectively having a +10/10 on the monoyer scale, also having another +5 on perception checks based on vision.
this effect is cumulative



Exceptional hearing

1 RCP

Requires: exceptional sense

This race have an exceptional hearing, giving them the ability to precisely locate persons up to 32m without watching, it also give them a +5 on perception throws based on hearing and +1 to sneakness.
Hearing sounds louder than 120db will give this race the effect scared, the decibels needed to get the effect stunned will be -10 for every time you took this trait.
This effect is cumulative


If you take this trait 5 or more times, you will have the ability to generally being able to form the shape of a small room in your mind, just by walking in it.
hearing sounds louder than 120db when having this trait 5 or more times will give this race the effect stunned, the decibels needed to get the effect stunned will be -10 for every time you took this trait (more than 5)



Exceptional smell

1 RCP

Requires: exceptional sense

Getting this trait will give the race the ability to be able to throw perception check based on smell, also from the second time you get this trait you will get a +2 on perception checks based on smell.
This effect is cumulative.



Fast

2 RCP



Add +3m to the character movement speed per turn.
This effect is cumulative



Fur

1 RCP



Choose between cold and hot climate this race get a folt layer of fur specifically made to protect in that kind of environment; Giving them a +5 resistance to temperature.
It also give the race a -2 resistance in the opposite environment.
This effect is cumulative but you can only choose one between cold and hot.



Glider

6 RCP



This race is built in a way that gives them the ability to glide but not fly (unless you get the trait wings, which give you this trait automatically).
You get the ability to move at 10x the walking speed in the air horizontally while falling at only 1m per round vertically.



Horns

4 RCP



This race posses an not specified number of horns which can be used to attack, dealing 1d6 + speed modifier damages



Invertebrate

-8 RCP



I genuenly do not know



Jumper

3 RCP



This species has the ability to jump really high, every time you take this trait, you add your height to max jump height.
This effect is cumulative.



Light weakness

-6 RCP



This race is not used to being exposed to light, so when in area with natural strong light it get the condition blinded, and if pointed directly with a strong light it get stunned.



Multiple arms

12 RCP



This species has multiple sets of prensile arms, which can be used to grab and manipulate objects.
This effect is cumulative



Multiple legs

4 RCP



This species has multiple sets of non prensile arms, which augment the stability giving a +4 on defense from falling and +3m per turn of movement speed.
This effect is cumulative except for the movement speed, which only applies once.



Natural armor

4 RCP



This species has a thick layer of skin, giving them +1 CA.
This effect is cumulative



No leg

-4 RCP



This species does not posses any legs and move in different ways (or don't move at all).
Other movemente types are the following:



Bouncer

4 RCP



This species does not posses any arts but can compress his body like a spring and then direct their jumps in the direction in which they want to move, this way they effectively can only move while jumping.
Movement speed is by default 3m per rounds, but you can take other traits, like fast to improve it; You cannot run.



Crawler

4 RCP



This species is not able to move with arts so they developed special techniques to crawl by moving their body, most commonly see in snake and invertebrates.
Your able to move by default at 1.5m per rounds; You can run at 4x your default speed.



Poisonous

5 RCP



Your body contains tossins that poison your enemies when they bite you, also your blood is poisonous so it can be used to craft Poison You can take this trait multiple times, damages and efficiency of the poison effect are cumulative.



Preferred terrain

2 RCP



This species is able to move particurarly well in a specific environment, for example monkeys or puma in a jungle, water does not count as an environment, for that you need another trait.
This species is not negatively affected when moving in that terrain
You can only choose one type of terrain



Shapeshifter

24 RCP



This species is able to partially or completely change it's aspect. It get a +30 to disguise and can benefit from the use of natural weapons, speed and other traits of the race it has turned into.
When shapeshifting into another race, it has to be of the same size



Sharp teeth

2 RCP



This species posses a very sharp set of teeth, used to bite and chop meet, having this trait automatically make the race able to eat raw meat without any risk.
You can take this trait up to to time, the first time the damage of the teeth will be 1d6 + (str/2), the second time it will become 1d8 + (str/2)



Strong jaw

3 RCP

Requires: sharp teeth

This species developed a very strong bite, giving them the ability to apply full strentgh modifier, instead of half whe calculating bite damage, and a bonus of +5 when fighting to keep the grip using their mouth.
It is also able to eat much harder meat.



Venomous

3 RCP

Requires: sharp teeth

This species produces a special toxin in their teeth, giving the effect poison to bitten enemies, 1d2 per round.
You can take this trait multiple times, damages and efficiency of the poison effect are cumulative.



Sleepless

3 RCP | -3 RCP



Originally I wanted to make that with this trait a race wouldn't need to sleep at all, but now I'm thinking more about something like for every time you take this trait you get one hour of sleep less per day, an you could also remove one level to get the point back but would need to sleep one hour more
I'm not sure if the basic should be 8h or 10h per night. Maybe between 8 to 10h cost nothing, but if you want to go lower you need to spend RCP or if you want to get more RCP you'll start from 10h, if that make sense.



Sleep vigil

4 RCP



This race only need to sleep partially, that means that it can sleep while performing very simple task (like walking if you're not a two legged creature) and it will also ignore the perception malus when sleeping.

Solar nourishment

15 RCP



With this trait you don't need to eat, but you need to stay in the direct sunlight for at least 10h per week or you'll start getting denutricion signs



Swim speed

8 RCP



This species posses fins for swimming underwater. Basic speed is 6m per round normal speed, doubled at running speed.



Tail

2 RCP



This species posses a movable tail (if you want a non movable tail it's free), it can be used to attack if you're size is at least 1, and cause 1d4*size non lethal damage



Multiple tails

1 RCP

Requires: tail

This species posses one more tail
this trait is cumulative



Spiker tail

6 RCP

Requires: tail

This species has some kind of spikes on their tales that let them attach enemies and cause 1d6 lethal damage
You can use this in combination with venomous, instead of picking sharp teeth



Strong tail

4 RCP

Requires: tail

This species has a very strong tail, you can choose between one of the following two functions, if you want both you can take the ability two times:
-Sustain full bodyweight on the tail
-Augment the non lethal damage caused by the tail, from a d4 to a d6 if you also took spiked tail trai, damaged will be summed and considered all lethal.



Telepathy

12 RCP



This species can communicate with other beings with the same species telepathically, sometimes even resulting in a one consciousness being, you cannot communicate telepathically with other species and by default you won't speak any language, this does not mean that you do not posses vocal chords, even if sometimes it's the case.



Temperature resistance

2 RCP



This species is more used to, or is specifically build in a way to resist well at different temperatures, giving them a +2 resistance in all temperature.
This trait is cumulative.



Dark vision

3 RCP



This species is able to see normally during the night, or in low light condition.



Wall crawler

6 RCP



This species arts, posses some kind of grip that let them walk freely on walls, like when they're walking on normal terrain, this does not help where the terrain is considered difficult condition, or if the wall is crumbly.



Water breathing

1 RCP



This species posses gills, that let them breath underwater normally.



Amphibian

2 RCP

Requires: water breathing

This species can breath underwater and out of the water, but instead of having a nose and gills it breath from the skin.
This species will always need to be wet and if not immersed in water at least once a day it will start dying.



Wings

10 RCP



This race posses wings and a lower body density, giving them the ability to flight at 10x the walking/running speed, the wings should be big enough to let you flight, if you want to fly with smaller wings you need to use blood magic.
Wings does not count when calculating size.



Scary wings

1 RCP

Requires: wings

This species use their open wings to scare others, giving them a +6 on intimidation.



Strong lungs

7 RCP

Requires: wings

This species is used to fly at very high altitude, being able to reach as much as 10Km from sea level, without getting the negative effects of the low oxigen.
