The project


I still need to choose a name for this gdr, but it's my own vision of tabletop RPG's, like dungeon's and dragons, but with my own spin on some of the mechanics. As much as I love the classics, there are small things I've always wanted to improve, so I’m redesigning certain aspects to make the experience smoother, more intuitive, and ultimately more fun.

I’ll be introducing completely new mechanics, as well as tweaking others, to create a system that feels more engaging and enjoyable. These changes reflect my personal vision for what I believe can make tabletop gaming even better.

In the long run, I’d love to make this project open-source, allowing others to contribute ideas and feedback. My goal is to build a community around it, where we can all collaborate and create something truly unique together.

Hope you will have fun playing it
